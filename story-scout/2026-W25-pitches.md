## Pitch 1 — The River of Reflections

**Story Bible thread:** Pukkai's Observations
**Premise:** While exploring the newly restored Creek Corridor in Juhu, Pukkai notices that the bioluminescent panels on Marine Drive are emitting a strange, pulsing glow at night. She can't explain why this is happening, but Putum's collar suggests it might be related to an unusual pattern of water currents and marine life behavior. As Pukkai delves deeper into the mystery, she uncovers evidence that some sections of the Creek Corridor may be hiding a forgotten, pre-restoration history.

**Key scenes:**
- Pukkai discovers the bioluminescent panels and shares her findings with Thatha Dipu.
- Putum's collar indicates that the creek's ecosystem is reacting to human presence in ways no one expected.
- Pukkai visits an old fisherman who remembers a long-abandoned canal system beneath the Creek Corridor.

**Mumbai location(s):** Juhu Creek, Marine Drive
**Why this fits the world:** The Creek Corridor and Marine Drive are integral to the comic's exploration of Mumbai's past, present, and future. This mystery highlights the complex relationship between technology and nature.
**Analogues referenced:** Research on bioluminescent organisms in marine environments.
